Enrollment & Reservations

To reserve your placement, please contact Lee directly. Due to our commitment to individualized instruction, all classes are limited to a maximum of four participants.

Class Structure & Attendance

  • Duration: Each session is 60 minutes, beginning with a guided warm-up and concluding with a Q&A period.
  • Punctuality: Please arrive and be settled 5 minutes prior to the scheduled start time.
  • Absences: One week notice prior to the start of sessions if you must drop.

Preparation & Attire

  • Clothing: Loose, comfortable clothing and soft-soled shoes; barefoot practice is welcomed.
  • Environment: Weather permitting, sessions will be held outdoors. Dress in layers.
  • Supplies: Please bring an exercise mat and water.

Tuition & Policies

  • Payment: Tuition is due in full on the first day of class via cash or check.
  • Refunds: No refunds or make-up sessions for individual absences.
  • Home Practice: Daily practice of 30–60 minutes is recommended.
